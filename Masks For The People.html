Case study:
Masks For The People.

Marketing Manager

Eric Davis Jr

Services

Branding, Content Creation,
Social Media Marketing

Date

Feburary 25th, 2020

Team

Jamal Dribble - Editor
Black Street Marketing - Video Animations | Editting | Marketing Strategy

Masks for the People is a nonprofit organization that provides high-quality masks to communities in need. With the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic, the organization faced a significant increase in demand for their services. However, they struggled to attract donations and support from the public. This is where Eric Davis Jr and Black Street Marketing came in to help.

Introduction. Challenge. Solution. Results.

Masks for the People needed to increase the visibility of their donor portal and attract more donations to support their mission. With limited resources and a small team, the organization needed an effective, efficient, and affordable solution. They also wanted to showcase their humanitarian efforts and create engaging content for their live streams.

Eric Davis Jr and his team at Black Street Marketing analyzed the organization's donor portal and identified areas for improvement. They developed a targeted social media campaign that included optimized posts and hashtags to increase visibility and awareness of the organization. The team also optimized the donor portal to make it more user-friendly and easy to navigate.

In addition, they created video content for Masks for the People's live streams. Notable guests who expressed their enthusiasm for the group's cause and urged viewers to contribute were featured in the videos. The videos were shared widely on social media and displayed on the organization's website, which significantly increased interest in and support for the group.

The alliance between Eric Davis Jr and Masks for the People was an outstanding success. The team reported a surge in visits to their donation portals, and a sizable portion of those visitors contributed to help them further their mission. The Masks for the People social media campaign was phenomenally effective, with thousands of people sharing and reposting information. The organization's initiatives received widespread media coverage, which broadened its visibility and audience. In a result, Masks for the People garnered over $10 million in donations, empowering them to broaden their initiatives and aid more deserving individuals.

Conclusion.

In conclusion, the collaboration between Masks for the People and Eric Davis Jr was instrumental in helping the organization increase its impact and attract support from the public. By implementing a targeted social media campaign and optimizing the donor portal, the organization was able to attract significant support and donations, which helped them to expand their initiatives and reach even more people in need. The success of this collaboration demonstrates the power of effective marketing and strategic planning in the nonprofit sector, and how it can lead to significant impact and positive change in communities. The video content created by Eric was also incredibly successful in showcasing the organization's efforts and generating support from the public.
